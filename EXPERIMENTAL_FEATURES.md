## üß™ Phase 1: Enhanced Learning (Beginner-Focused)

### 1. **Interactive Trading Lessons**
**What:** Built-in educational modules with quizzes
- Lesson topics: Risk management, position sizing, R:R ratios, reading charts
- After each lesson, unlock features
- Progress tracking: "5/20 lessons completed"
- Gamification: Earn badges for completed lessons
- **Why:** Beginners need education integrated with their tools

### 2. **Trading Simulator / Paper Trading Mode**
**What:** Practice mode with fake money
- Toggle "Practice Mode" to simulate trades
- Use real market data (if API available) or manual price input
- Track simulated P&L separately from real trades
- Compare sim performance to real performance
- Graduate from sim when hitting milestones (50% win rate for 20 trades, etc.)
- **Why:** Beginners can practice without risking money

### 3. **Mentor Mode / Trade Review Assistant**
**What:** AI acts as a trading mentor
- Before trade entry: AI reviews your plan and warns about issues
- "Are you sure?" prompts when breaking rules
- Post-trade: Mandatory review questions
- Weekly review sessions with AI feedback
- **Why:** Beginners need guidance and accountability

### 4. **Risk Calculator Tool**
**What:** Built-in position sizing calculator
- Input: Account size, risk %, entry, stop loss
- Output: Position size, dollar risk, R:R visualization
- Save common setups as templates
- Warning if risk exceeds settings
- **Why:** Beginners struggle with position sizing math

### 5. **Trading Psychology Journal**
**What:** Separate journal for mental state
- Daily mood check-in before trading
- Reflection prompts: "What am I feeling today?"
- Correlation: Map emotions to trading outcomes
- Warning system: "You've traded angry 3 times this week, all losses"
- **Why:** Psychology is 80% of trading success

## üöÄ Phase 2: Advanced Analytics

### 6. **Machine Learning Pattern Recognition**
**What:** ML model finds patterns in your data
- Identifies: "You lose 70% when trading before 10 AM on Mondays"
- Discovers hidden correlations
- Predicts win probability for new trade based on historical patterns
- Suggests optimal trade times, symbols, conditions
- **Why:** Humans miss patterns that algorithms catch

### 7. **Monte Carlo Simulation**
**What:** Statistical modeling of future performance
- Run 1,000 simulations of next 100 trades
- Show probability distributions of outcomes
- Risk of ruin calculation
- Optimal position sizing via Kelly Criterion
- **Why:** Understand long-term probabilities

### 8. **Drawdown Analyzer**
**What:** Deep analysis of losing periods
- Visualize drawdown curves
- Identify triggers (market conditions, emotions, time)
- Recovery time analysis
- Comparison to historical drawdowns
- **Why:** Learn from the worst periods to prevent repeats

### 9. **Correlation Matrix**
**What:** Find relationships between variables
- Which emotions correlate with losses?
- Do certain symbols correlate with each other?
- Time of day vs market condition interactions
- Heatmap visualization
- **Why:** Understand complex relationships in your data

### 10. **Backtesting Engine**
**What:** Test strategies on historical data
- Upload CSV of historical prices
- Run strategy rules against data
- See how it would have performed
- Optimize parameters
- **Why:** Validate strategies before risking money

## üîî Phase 4: Automation & Alerts

### 15. **Smart Alerts System**
**What:** Proactive notifications
- "You've lost 3 in a row - time for a break"
- "Max daily loss limit approaching"
- "You're on a 5-trade win streak - stay focused"
- "It's your best trading time (10 AM-12 PM)"
- Customizable alert rules
- **Why:** Real-time guidance prevents mistakes

### 16. **Automated Trade Import**
**What:** Import from broker via API or CSV
- Connect broker account (TradingView, Interactive Brokers, etc.)
- Auto-import executed trades
- Reduce manual entry errors
- Still allows manual edits/notes
- **Why:** Saves time, ensures accuracy

### 17. **Pre-Trade Checklist Enforcer**
**What:** Block trade entry until checklist complete
- Hard mode: Can't submit trade form until 100% checklist done
- Soft mode: Warning popup but can proceed
- Track bypass rate
- **Why:** Forces good habits

### 18. **Risk Limit Enforcer**
**What:** Prevent over-trading and excessive risk
- Set hard limits: Max 3 trades/day, max 2% risk/trade
- App blocks new trades when limit hit
- Override requires reason + password
- Track violations
- **Why:** Protects beginners from themselves
